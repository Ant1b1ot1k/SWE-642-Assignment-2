Description:

Welcome to the Computer Science Department website! Our department is dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive education in computer science that prepares them for successful careers in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. The College of Engineering and Computing ranked 44th in online graduate programs in the 2023 U.S. News and World Report rankings.

MS Degrees:

We offer a variety of MS degree programs to suit the individual interests and goals of our students. Our programs include:

  • Computer Science, MS
  • Artificial Intelligence, MS
  • Data Science, MS
  • Cybersecurity, MS

For each program, we provide a rigorous curriculum that combines both theoretical and practical coursework. Below are the required courses for each MS degree program:
